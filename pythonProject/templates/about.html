Dan Gribbin - About Me
A Musical Journey

With the release here in the summer of 2018 of the new CD, "Florida in My Soul," I find myself surrounded by musical friends every weekend. The Unitarian Universalist Congregation of Ormond Beach, where I have a hand in the programming, is the site of much of this activity. But the Will McLean Festival puts me in touch with fellow fans of Pete Seeger, who have encouraged me to record "Pete Seeger in Paradise" on the new album. And I must put in a plug for the Lake County Folk Festival, where "The Deadbeat" won Best Funny Song, last fall. Please give a listen to "A Friend Like You," where Al Scortino adds his terrific dobro. It's a full musical life!
